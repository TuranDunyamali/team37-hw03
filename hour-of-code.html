Hour of Code event

Details of our event:

  • Date: November 4th
  • Location: XXI Century School
  • Duration: 55 minutes
  • Participants: 5th grade students
  • Number of participants: 20 students

Activities:

Aiming to familiarize children with the basics of coding, our team 37 organized an Hour of code. We began with a brief introduction to ADA University and our school, followed by an explanation of how to code using the Blockly language. After launching the Classic Maze Puzzle on each computer, we officially kicked off our event. Throughout the Hour of Code, we actively assisted students with their challenges and addressed their questions. After all students completed the puzzle, we handed out the certificates.
